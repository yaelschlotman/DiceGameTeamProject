Welcome to 'Walking Simulator 2016'

Experience the Intensity of Walking!

Game description

You are a regular old human, and you need to find a way to walk, that's right! One foot in front of the other, how hard could all of that be? That's correct, it's very hard. You have to overcome obstacles to get to the end of the board, to overcome you need to meet certain requirements for dice rolls. So Keep Calm and Walk On!

Game Rules

Obstacles: Roll must be at least:
Stump 4!
Rock 8!
Manhole 12!
Fence 16!
Win Condition Be first to overcome final obstacle!
